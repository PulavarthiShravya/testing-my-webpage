About Little Mix

Little Mix is a British girl group formed in 2011. They were the first group to win the 'X-Factor' competition. Later on they signed with Simon Cowell's record label Syco Music and released a cover of Damien Rice's "Cannonball" as their winner's single, which debuted atop the UK Singles Chart upon release

About Little Mix Members

Jade Amelia Thirwall

Thirwall was born on 26 December 1992. She was born and raised in Laygate area of South Shields, Tyne and Wear, England.She is a mixed blood of Egyptian & Yemeni [Mother] and English [Father]. She appeared on the X-Factor show in 2008 along with her other Little Mix members. Her favorite color is Teal and Purple. She loves to eat Lasange and biscuits.

Perrie Louise Edwards

Edwardswas born on 10 July 1993. She was born South Shields, Tyne and Wear, England. She appeared on the X-Factor show in 2008 along with her other Little Mix members. Her favorite color is blue. She loves to indulge herself in Tacos.

Leigh-Anne Pinnock

Leigh-Anne Pinnock was born on 4 October 1991. She was raised in High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire, England. Pinnock is of Barbadian and Jamaican ancestry.She appeared on the X-Factor show in 2008 along with her other Little Mix members. Her favorite color is green. She loves to eat Nachos.

Jesy Lousie Nelson

Jessica Louise Nelson was born on 14 June 1991. She was raised in Romford, East London, England. She appeared on the X-Factor show in 2008 along with her other Little Mix members. Her favorite color is pink. She loves to eat is Nando's.
